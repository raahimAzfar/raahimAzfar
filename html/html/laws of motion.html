Newton's laws of motion

Sir Issac newton discovered gravity and
made the three laws of motion.His name is used
for the SI unit of force.



Newton's first law of motion



An object will remain in motion if in motion
And an object will remain to restive in rest
unless acted on by net force.



basically, if something is moving/still it will
keep going/staying still until a force interacts.




Newton's second law of motion



The acceleration of an object is equal to the
net force on an object divided by mass of
the object.



this is the math equation for that



        
acceleration=net force ÷ mass




Newton's third law of motion



Forces act in pairs:For every action
there is an equal but opposite reaction




e.g. when you jump on a trampoline,
the trampoline pushes you back up
